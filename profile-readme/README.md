## About Me

I am an oceanography graduate and current MSc student at Bangladesh Maritime University, with research interests in **water quality, coastal ecosystems, river ecosystem health, climate change, potential fishing zones, GIS/remote sensing, and environmental data analysis**.

I support research teams through literature review, systematic-review-style article screening, field and laboratory data handling, Python/SPSS-based analysis, GIS mapping, technical documentation, proposal support, and manuscript preparation.
